Team building across Schmoll Asia Pacific

Colleagues from engineering, service, sales and operations came together for a day of sport and team spirit.

Precision On And Off The Court

Team spirit was in full focus at our latest Schmoll Asia Pacific team-building event.

Colleagues from engineering, service, sales, operations and facility support came together for a day of basketball, barbecue and great conversations beyond daily work.

Events like this remind us that strong performance starts with strong teamwork. Whether on the production floor, in customer service or on the court, precision and collaboration are what move us forward.

A big thank you to everyone who joined and helped make the day a memorable one.
