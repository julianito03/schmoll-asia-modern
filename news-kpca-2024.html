KPCA Show 2024: innovation and collaboration

Presenting PCB drilling technology to the Korean market at KPCA Show 2024.

Event 25 Sep 2024·2 min read

Schmoll Asia Pacific’s Team at KPCA Show 2024

We are excited to announce that Schmoll Asia Pacific had a highly successful participation at the KPCA Show 2024, held from September 4th to 6th. Our presence at the show not only showcased our innovative PCB drilling machines but also reinforced our commitment to delivering industry-leading precision machinery across the Asia Pacific region.

  • Innovative PCB Drilling Solutions: During the show, we presented live demonstrations of our latest PCB drillingand manufacturing machines, demonstrating the advanced technology and precision that have made Schmoll Asia Pacific a trusted name in the industry.
  • Industry Networking: We had the privilege of engaging with key players from the PCB industry and fostering partnerships that will help drive further growth and innovation.
  • Customer Interaction: Our booth welcomed a steady stream of visitors, including current customers and potential partners, all eager to learn how Schmoll Asia Pacific continues to provide top-notch service and innovative solutions tailored to the needs of the region.

As the demand for PCB manufacturing continues to grow, Schmoll Asia Pacific remains at the forefront, pushing boundaries with cutting-edge PCB drilling technologies. Our success at KPCA Show 2024 underscores our role as an industry leader in the region, providing solutions that increase efficiency, reduce errors, and improve overall production capabilities.

By consistently delivering high-precision PCB machinery and dedicated customer service, we are poised to continue leading the market in PCB manufacturing and automation solutions.

Schmoll Asia Pacific’s engineers

We would like to thank all the industry professionals, partners, and clients who visited our booth at the KPCA Show 2024. Your support and interest in our PCB drilling machines and solutions are what drives us to keep innovating.

Stay connected with us for more updates and exciting advancements in the world of PCB technology. Schmoll Asia Pacific is dedicated to supporting the growing needs of the PCB manufacturing industry and ensuring top-notch service for our top-notch machines.
