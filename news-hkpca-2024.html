Schmoll Asia Pacific Concludes a Successful Year at the HKPCA Show 2024

18 Dec 2024News All news

Schmoll Asia Pacific has successfully wrapped up its participation at the HKPCA Show 2024, held from December 4th to 6th at the Shenzhen International Exhibition Center (Bao’an). This event marked the final exhibition of the year for our team, and what a remarkable way to close out 2024!

At our booth 7C40, we had the privilege of showcasing our industry-leading solutions to key players in the PCB manufacturing sector. This year’s exhibition allowed us to connect with customers, partners, and innovators from around the world, discussing new opportunities and challenges in PCB manufacturing.

The excitement and energy shared at the show reaffirmed our belief that the PCB manufacturing industry is advancing at an unprecedented rate. As always, we remain focused on leading the industry with our cutting-edge products. We continue to innovate, provide solutions that address the most complex manufacturing needs, and support our customers in achieving the highest standards of quality and precision.

Looking ahead to 2025, we are determined to continue this momentum and enter the new year with the same goal: to lead the PCB manufacturing industry. We are already planning for new product launches, more industry partnerships, and innovative developments that will help shape the future of the industry.

A heartfelt thank you to everyone who visited us at the show and to those who have supported us throughout this year. We look forward to even greater achievements in 2025 as we push forward, break new ground, and continue to be at the forefront of the PCB manufacturing world.

金富宝亚太 在 2024 HKPCA 展会圆满结束：引领 PCB 制造创新

金富宝亚太 已成功结束了在 2024 HKPCA 展会的参展，展会于 12 月 4 日至 6 日在深圳国际会展中心（宝安）举行。这次展会标志着我们团队今年的最后一次展出，作为 2024 年的完美收官，我们感到无比自豪！

在我们的 7C40 展位，我们有幸向 PCB 制造行业的重要参与者展示我们的领先解决方案。今年的展会让我们有机会与来自世界各地的客户、合作伙伴和创新者交流，探讨 PCB 制造中的新机遇和挑战。

展会期间的兴奋氛围和创新精神让我们更加坚定了对 PCB 制造行业快速发展的信心。我们始终专注于通过我们的尖端产品引领行业。我们将继续创新，提供解决方案，满足最复杂的制造需求，并支持客户实现最高的质量和精度标准。

展望 2025，我们决心继续保持势头，进入新的一年时依旧坚持我们的目标：引领 PCB 制造行业。我们已经在筹划新的产品发布、更多的行业合作伙伴关系和创新发展，帮助塑造 PCB 行业的未来。

衷心感谢所有参观我们展位的朋友们，也感谢所有在这一年中支持我们的伙伴。我们期待在 2025 年取得更加辉煌的成就，推动行业发展，继续站在 PCB 制造领域的最前沿。
