HKPCA 2025: 30 years of Schmoll in Asia

HKPCA 2025 in Shenzhen closed the exhibition year and marked three decades of Schmoll activity in the region.

Event 09 Dec 2025·2 min read

HKPCA 2025 in Shenzhen was more than the last exhibition of the year for Schmoll Maschinen and Schmoll Asia Pacific. It was a live demonstration of why customers across the region trust Schmoll for some of the best PCB equipment in Asia and high-precision PCB drilling machines, and the perfect stage to celebrate 30 years of Schmoll Asia Pacific.

From the first crate opened to the final handshake, visitors experienced what matters most in PCB production: accuracy, throughput, automation and strong local support.

At booth 7C20, Schmoll Asia Pacific presented a range of solutions that many PCB manufacturers in Asia rely on for demanding production environments:

  • High-precision PCB drilling machines for HDI, multilayer, AI servers and advanced applications
  • Routing systems designed for clean edges, tight tolerances and repeatable quality
  • Automation options to increase throughput, reduce handling and stabilise processes

Customers could discuss their specific PCB production needs for 2026 and beyond, and see how Schmoll technology supports:

  • Higher spindle speeds and positioning accuracy
  • Stable drilling processes for fine holes and complex stack-ups
  • Integration into highly automated PCB production lines

HKPCA 2025 marked a major milestone: 30 years of Schmoll Asia Pacific supporting PCB manufacturers across the region.

Over three decades, Schmoll Asia Pacific has:

  • Built local technical and service teams close to customers in key PCB clusters
  • Supported multiple technology generations, from conventional boards to today’s fine-line, high-density structures
  • Helped customers choose and implement some of the best PCB drilling machines and routing solutions in Asia
  • Delivered long-term process support so that equipment keeps performing at the highest level

This anniversary is about more than time. It represents long-standing customer relationships, shared projects and continuous improvement in PCB production technology.

With HKPCA 2025 wrapped up and 30 years of Schmoll Asia Pacific celebrated, the focus is firmly on the future.

Together with Schmoll Maschinen and the global group companies, Schmoll Asia Pacific will continue to:

  • Develop next-generation PCB drilling machines and routing systems
  • Expand automation and digitalisation options for modern PCB factories
  • Invest in local service, applications and process expertise across Asia
  • Support customers in building efficient, competitive PCB production lines

The last exhibition of the year may be over, but the next chapter has already begun.

For PCB manufacturers looking for the best PCB equipment in Asia, especially high-precision PCB drilling machines backed by strong local support, Schmoll Asia Pacific remains a trusted partner for the years ahead.

亚洲最佳 PCB 设备供应商 —— Schmoll Asia Pacific 在 HKPCA 2025 庆祝 30 周年

亚洲最佳 PCB 设备精彩亮相

在深圳举办的 HKPCA 2025，不仅是 Schmoll Maschinen 和 金富宝亚太（Schmoll Asia Pacific） 年度收官之展，更是一次现场证明：为什么众多客户将 Schmoll 视为亚洲最佳 PCB 设备及高精度 PCB 钻孔机合作伙伴——同时，这也是 金富宝亚太成立 30 周年 的重要节点。

从展台搭建的第一颗螺丝，到展会结束前的最后一次握手，来访嘉宾都近距离感受到 PCB 生产真正关注的核心：精度、产能、自动化以及强有力的本地支持。

在 7C20 展位，金富宝亚太集中展示了多款被亚洲 PCB 制造商广泛采用的高端 PCB 设备：

  • 面向 HDI、多层板及先进应用的高精度 PCB 钻孔机
  • 追求光滑边缘、紧公差与高重复性的成型（Routing）系统
  • 用于提升产能、减少人工搬运、稳定制程的自动化解决方案

通过现场交流，客户可以围绕 2026 年及未来的产线规划，与团队一起探讨如何通过 Schmoll 设备实现：

  • 更高主轴转速与精准定位
  • 适用于微孔及复杂叠构的稳定钻孔工艺
  • 与高度自动化 PCB 生产线的高效集成

这些能力，是 Schmoll 被视为亚洲最佳 PCB 钻孔机供应商和领先 PCB 设备品牌的重要基础。

HKPCA 2025 同时见证了 金富宝亚太服务亚洲 PCB 行业 30 周年 的里程碑。

三十年来，金富宝亚太一直致力于：

  • 在关键 PCB 产业集群搭建本地技术与售后服务团队
  • 穿越多代技术演进——从传统线路板到今天的细线、高密度与高可靠性应用
  • 帮助客户导入并优化使用高精度 PCB 钻孔机与成型设备
  • 通过长期工艺与应用支持，确保设备在全生命周期内稳定运行、发挥价值

这 30 年，不只是时间长度，更是与客户共同成长、共同升级制程与产线的真实记录。

在圆满收官 HKPCA 2025 并庆祝金富宝亚太 30 周年之后，我们的目光已经投向未来。

与 Schmoll Maschinen 及全球集团公司一道，金富宝亚太将持续：

  • 推出新一代 PCB 钻孔机与成型系统
  • 扩展 自动化与数字化 解决方案，助力打造现代化 PCB 工厂
  • 加强在亚洲各主要市场的本地服务与应用支持
  • 帮助客户打造更高效、更具竞争力的 PCB 生产能力

对于正在寻找亚洲最佳 PCB 设备与高精度 PCB 钻孔机的制造企业而言，金富宝亚太 将继续是值得信赖的长期合作伙伴。
