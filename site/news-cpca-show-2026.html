CPCA Show 2026 | Best PCB Equipment for AI Data servers

06 Apr 2026Blog All news

Schmoll Asia Pacific has successfully concluded its participation at CPCA Show 2026. The exhibition once again provided an excellent platform to connect with customers, partners, and industry experts from across the PCB manufacturing sector.

The event created valuable opportunities to discuss current market developments, future requirements, and the technologies that will shape the next generation of PCB production.

A key topic at this year’s show was the rising demand for complex PCBs for AI data servers. As AI infrastructure continues to expand, PCB manufacturers are facing increasing requirements for precision, performance, and process reliability.

Advanced server applications require highly accurate drilling, controlled back-drilling, fine structures, and stable imaging performance. These demands are driving a new level of complexity in PCB manufacturing, and Schmoll is committed to delivering the technology needed to meet them.

Schmoll continues to focus on solutions that support the production of high-performance PCBs with maximum precision and efficiency. Our technologies are designed to help manufacturers address the most demanding production challenges while maintaining the highest quality standards.

The strong interest and valuable discussions during CPCA Show 2026 once again confirmed that the industry is moving quickly, with innovation and precision becoming more important than ever.

To support future growth, Schmoll Maschinen is currently building a new factory in Germany to significantly increase machine output.

This investment is an important step in strengthening our production capabilities and meeting the growing international demand for advanced PCB manufacturing technology. It will also help us better support Asia’s increasing requirements for high-end PCB production solutions.

We sincerely thank everyone who visited our team at CPCA Show 2026. We appreciate the many valuable conversations and look forward to continuing to shape the future of PCB manufacturing together.

面向 AI 数据服务器时代 推动 PCB 技术持续发展 CPCA SHOW 2026

金富宝亚太圆满完成了此次 CPCA Show 2026 之行。此次展会再次为我们提供了一个宝贵的平台，与来自 PCB 制造领域的客户、合作伙伴以及行业专家展开深入交流。

展会期间，我们围绕当前市场发展趋势、未来技术需求，以及下一代 PCB 制造所需的关键工艺进行了广泛探讨。

本届展会的一大重点，是AI 数据服务器对高复杂度 PCB 日益增长的需求。随着 AI 基础设施持续扩展，PCB 制造商正面临更高的精度、性能和工艺稳定性要求。

先进服务器应用对高精度钻孔、可控背钻、精细线路以及稳定成像能力提出了更高标准。这些要求不断提升 PCB 制造的复杂度，而金富宝始终致力于提供能够应对这些挑战的先进技术解决方案。

金富宝持续专注于支持高性能 PCB 制造的解决方案，帮助客户在实现更高精度与生产效率的同时，从容应对严苛的制造挑战，并始终保持卓越的品质标准。

在 CPCA Show 2026 期间，我们感受到市场的高度关注，也收获了许多有价值的交流。这再次印证了整个行业正加速迈向更高的创新水平与更高的精密制造要求。

为支持未来发展，金富宝机械目前正在德国建设全新工厂，以显著提升设备产能。

这, 重要投资将进, 步增强我们的生产制造能力，满足全球市场对先进 PCB 制造技术不断增长的需求，同时也将更有力地支持亚洲市场对高端 PCB 生产解决方案的持续增长需求。

衷心感谢所有在 CPCA Show 2026 期间莅临金富宝亚太展位的来宾。感谢每一次宝贵交流，期待未来与各位继续携手合作，共同推动 PCB 制造行业不断向前发展。
