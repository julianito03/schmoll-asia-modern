Successful Participation at CPCA Show 2025 in Shanghai

22 Apr 2025News All news

Schmoll Asia Pacific is proud to look back on an inspiring and successful presence at the CPCA Show 2025, held from March 24–26 at the NECC in Shanghai. Over the course of three dynamic days, we had the pleasure of showcasing our latest innovations and cutting-edge solutions at Booth 7D38, connecting with industry leaders, partners, and visitors from across the PCB and electronics manufacturing sector.

The event provided an excellent platform to engage in meaningful conversations, share insights, and explore future collaboration opportunities. We were thrilled by the positive feedback and overwhelming interest in the technologies we presented.

Thank you to everyone who visited our booth and contributed to making this event such a success. Your enthusiasm and support continue to drive our passion for innovation and excellence.

As we reflect on these energizing days, we are excited about the new relationships established and look forward to what the future holds. Stay tuned for more updates as we continue to shape the future of PCB manufacturing together.

金富宝亚太圆满收官 2025 CPCA 展会之旅

金富宝亚太在**2025 年中国电子电路展览会（CPCA Show 2025）**上的精彩亮相圆满落幕。展会于 3 月 24 日至 26 日在上海国家会展中心（NECC）举行，三天的交流与展示让我们收获颇丰。

在 7D38 展位，我们展示了金富宝亚太最新的创新技术与解决方案，吸引了来自 PCB 及电子制造行业的众多专家和合作伙伴前来交流。感谢每一位莅临我们展位的朋友，是您们的热情与支持让此次展会更加精彩。

展会期间的深入交流不仅让我们分享了行业见解，也为未来的合作机会奠定了坚实的基础。此次展会带给我们的启发与能量，让我们更加期待与各位在未来共同探索更多可能。

金富宝亚太将继续秉持创新精神，不断推动行业发展。敬请关注我们，携手共创美好未来！
