Success at TPCA Show 2024

Meeting Taiwan's PCB industry at TPCA Show 2024.

Event 07 Nov 2024·1 min read

Schmoll Asia Pacific’s Team at the Booth

The TPCA Show 2024 kicked off with great momentum, and Schmoll Asia Pacific was thrilled to join the industry’s best in Taipei, marking yet another step forward in PCB technology innovation. From October 23rd to 25th, the Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center transformed into a hub of industry leaders, enthusiasts, and tech pioneers—all gathering to explore the future of PCB manufacturing.

At booth L-722, Schmoll Asia Pacific presented a range of advanced solutions in PCB manufacturing. Our team demonstrated cutting-edge precision tools and production technology aimed at driving efficiency, quality, and sustainable practices across the industry. Each interaction at our booth highlighted our commitment to innovation and excellence, solidifying Schmoll Asia Pacific’s role as a leader in the field.

Cutting-edge Technology Made in Germany – Drilling Machine Falcon

  • Inspiring Innovation: Visitors got a first-hand look at our latest equipment designed for high-frequency and multi-layer PCBs, addressing the growing need for high-performance, reliable production.
  • Expert Engagement: Our experts were on hand to discuss each product’s specific benefits and provide insights into industry trends.
  • New Solutions Unveiled: The show served as an ideal platform to introduce our newest offerings, from enhanced drill systems to smart automation solutions, all tailored to improve productivity and quality for our clients.

Whether you visited to explore solutions or seek industry insights, we’re here to continue the conversation. Find out more about how Schmoll Asia Pacific is transforming PCB manufacturing for the future

金富寶亞太有限公司於 2024 年 TPCA 展取得成功

金富寶亞太有限公司展台團隊

2024 年 TPCA 展會盛大開幕，金富寶亞太有限公司非常榮幸與業內頂尖企業齊聚台北，共同推動 PCB 技術創新的又一里程碑。10 月 23 日至 25 日，台北南港展覽館成為行業領導者、愛好者和技術先鋒的聚集地，大家匯聚一堂，共同探索 PCB 製造的未來。

在 L-722 展台，金富寶亞太有限公司展示了一系列先進的 PCB 製造解決方案。我們的團隊展示了尖端的精密工具和生產技術，旨在提升行業的效率、品質和可持續發展。展台上的每次交流都彰顯了我們對創新與卓越的承諾，進一步鞏固了金富寶亞太有限公司作為行業領導者的地位。

德國製造的尖端技術 – 鑽孔機 Falcon

  • 激發創新：參觀者親眼見證了我們最新設備的展示，專為高頻和多層 PCB 設計，以滿足對高性能、可靠生產的日益需求。
  • 專家互動：我們的專家現場解答每款產品的特定優勢，並分享行業趨勢的洞見。
  • 全新解決方案發布：展會成為我們推出最新產品的理想平台，包括升級版鑽孔系統和智能自動化解決方案，所有產品均旨在提高客戶的生產力和品質。

金富宝亚太展台团队
