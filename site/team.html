Meet our Team

Schmoll Group CEO

Thomas Kunz

Thomas Kunz stepped up to lead Schmoll Maschinen GmbH at the age of 33 when the company was struggling with insolvency. Over the last 32 years, his visionary leadership and steadfast determination have driven Schmoll to become a leader in the PCB industry.

His clear vision and innovative thinking helped the company overcome tough times and achieve success. His deep knowledge of the industry, dedication to continuous learning, and strong ethical standards have inspired both employees and stakeholders.

With his guidance, Schmoll has not only grown significantly but also set new benchmarks for excellence and sustainability — a remarkable journey that highlights his exceptional leadership and lasting impact on the company.

Schmoll Asia Pacific CEO — Our Leader

Joachim Ruff

“With 35 years of experience in running businesses and 30 years in the PCB industry, I consider myself the silverback of the company.

My aim is to build a promising future for the next generation. Happy customers are my pride, and with our professional team, we can achieve great goals together.

I take pleasure in traveling across the Asian market to meet customers and understand their needs, learning from every interaction. We are competing in the Champions League of our market, and I am proud to contribute to our strategic success as part of this exceptional team.”

Joachim Ruff
