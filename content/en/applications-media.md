This page has more real body text than the other application pages:

**About / Mission:**
The company has offered "German quality and Innovation" in Asia since 1996, operating as
a leading PCB drilling and routing machinery provider. Precision-engineering features
cited: "high-speed linear motors, granite machine bases for superior stability, and
sophisticated tool adjustment systems."

**Global Presence:**
Schmoll Asia Pacific maintains 8 offices across 7 countries: China (2 locations), Taiwan,
Malaysia, Thailand, Korea, Hong Kong, and Vietnam.

**80-Year Timeline of Innovations:**
- 1943: Founded by Heinz Schmoll in Germany
- 1962: First European PCB drilling machine
- 1985: Twin concept (dual spindles)
- 1992: Contact Bit Detection (CBD) system
- 1999: Linear motor technology
- 2004: MXY-CCD concept for precision drilling
- 2006: 300k sub spindle (up to 300,000 RPM)
- 2015: Laser drilling technology
- 2023: X-Ray inspection systems
