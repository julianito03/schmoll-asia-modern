NOTE on machine images: The individual product pages do NOT contain dedicated
standalone machine photos — they only carry the site logo, a generic header
banner (`SAP-Korea-6`), and a WeChat QR code. The most machine-like image
available for each model is the **front cover of its product brochure**, served
by the site as a rendered JPG (`...-pdf.jpg`). These covers show the machine
render/photo, so they are used as each machine's image. Marked "brochure-cover
render" below (i.e. a fallback, not a clean isolated product photo).
