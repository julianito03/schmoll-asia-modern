Schmoll technology on show at CPCA 2024

Drilling, routing and automation technology presented to PCB manufacturers at CPCA 2024.

Event 26 Nov 2024·1 min read

Schmoll Asia Pacific is proud to announce another milestone in the PCB industry as we successfully showcased our cutting-edge technology at the CPCA 2024 Electronics Circuit Exhibition. Held at the Shenzhen World Exhibition & Convention Center from November 6 to November 8, 2024, the event was a testament to innovation, collaboration, and the relentless pursuit of precision.

Our booth became a focal point for attendees eager to experience the future of PCB drilling and routing firsthand. Visitors were captivated by live demonstrations of Schmoll Maschinen’s flagship technologies, including advanced CCD camera alignment systems, non-linear compensation for X/Y optimization, and smart mapping solutions. These innovations set new benchmarks for efficiency and accuracy in electronics manufacturing.

Throughout the exhibition, our team at Schmoll Asia Pacific engaged with professionals across the electronics industry. Conversations centered on industry challenges and the transformative impact of Schmoll Maschinen’s solutions. It was inspiring to see the enthusiasm and forward-thinking spirit shared by attendees from across the globe.

  • Interactive Technology Showcase: Attendees explored our latest PCB solutions, with a focus on high-frequency backdrilling and Z-axis precision optimization.
  • Expert Insights: Our team shared valuable knowledge on integrating Schmoll Maschinen technology into existing manufacturing systems to maximize output and reduce operational costs.
  • Future Vision: Discussions emphasized the importance of staying ahead in a rapidly evolving market, with Schmoll Maschinen at the forefront of this transition.

We extend our heartfelt thanks to everyone who visited Booth 8C02. Your questions, ideas, and feedback drive us to continuously improve and innovate.

As we reflect on the success of CPCA 2024, Schmoll Asia Pacific remains committed to supporting the industry with groundbreaking solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our partners.
